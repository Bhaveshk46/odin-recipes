Vada-pav

Description:

The above image shows a classic Vada Pav, the ultimate local street food snack from Mumbai. It features a golden, deep-fried spiced potato dumpling (vada) tucked inside a soft, sliced bread roll (pav). The pav is layered with a vibrant green chutney and a bold red garlic masala for a sharp, spicy kick. It is served with a fried green chili on top and extra crispy bits of chickpea flour batter scattered around for added texture.

Ingredients:

  • Potato
  • Chickpea Flour
  • Green Chilies
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Asafoetida
    •

Steps:

  1. Boil Potatoes Cook and mash the potatoes to prepare the vada base.
  2. Temper Spices: Saute mustard seeds, asafoetida, ginger, garlic, and green chilies.
  3. Mix Filling: Combine the tempered spices with mashed potatoes, turmeric, and fresh coriander.
  4. Prepare Batter: Whisk chickpea flour with turmeric, salt, and water to a thick consistency.
  5. Shape and Fry: Form potato balls, dip them in the batter, and deep-fry until golden.
  6. Prepare Pav: Slice the bread rolls and spread green chutney and red garlic masala inside.
  7. Assemble: Place the fried vada inside the pav and add crispy batter bits.
  8. Serve: Garnish with a fried green chili on top.
